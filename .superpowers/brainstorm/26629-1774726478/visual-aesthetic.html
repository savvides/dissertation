What visual aesthetic should we use?

For a dissertation summary, how should the UI look and feel?

A

Academic Minimalist

Clean, stark, and data-focused. Serif typography for headers (like academic papers), a stark black-and-white color scheme, and very subtle, precise chart lines. High signal, low noise.

B

Modern Tech Blog / Medium

Accessible and engaging. Clean sans-serif typography (like Inter or Roboto), generous whitespace, and a cohesive, modern color palette (e.g., slate grays with a bright accent color for key data points).

C

"Dark Mode" VR Aesthetic

A nod to the subject matter. A dark background (deep charcoal or navy) with bright, neon accents (cyan, magenta) for the charts. Feels immersive and slightly futuristic.
